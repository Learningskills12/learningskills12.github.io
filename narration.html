📚 What is Narration Change?

Narration Change হলো এমন একটি Grammar Topic যেখানে একজন ব্যক্তির বলা কথা (Speech) অন্য একজনের মাধ্যমে একই অর্থ বজায় রেখে প্রকাশ করা হয়।

Narration Change-এর প্রধান উদ্দেশ্য হলো Direct Speech-কে Indirect Speech-এ অথবা Indirect Speech-কে Direct Speech-এ পরিবর্তন করা।

💡 Definition

Direct Speech: যখন বক্তার কথাকে ঠিক যেমন বলা হয়েছে, উদ্ধৃতি চিহ্ন (" ") ব্যবহার করে লেখা হয়।


Indirect Speech: যখন বক্তার কথাকে উদ্ধৃতি চিহ্ন ছাড়া নিজের ভাষায় প্রকাশ করা হয়।

📌 Structure

Speech Structure
Direct Speech Reporting Verb + "Reported Speech"
Indirect Speech Reporting Verb + Reported Speech

📝 Direct Speech Examples

  1. Rahim said, "I am happy."
  2. Mina said, "I like English."
  3. He said, "I play football."
  4. She said, "I read books."
  5. Father said, "Work hard."
  6. Teacher said, "Be honest."
  7. Mother said, "Drink milk."
  8. He said, "The sun rises in the east."
  9. They said, "We are ready."
  10. She said, "I can swim."

📝 Indirect Speech Examples

  1. Rahim said that he was happy.
  2. Mina said that she liked English.
  3. He said that he played football.
  4. She said that she read books.
  5. Father advised me to work hard.
  6. The teacher advised us to be honest.
  7. Mother advised me to drink milk.
  8. He said that the sun rises in the east.
  9. They said that they were ready.
  10. She said that she could swim.

⭐ Important Points

  • ✅ Narration দুই প্রকার।
  • ✅ Direct Speech-এ quotation (" ") থাকে।
  • ✅ Indirect Speech-এ quotation থাকে না।
  • ✅ Reporting Verb খুব গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।
  • ✅ Reporting Speech-এর Tense পরিবর্তন হতে পারে।
  • ✅ Pronoun পরিবর্তন হতে পারে।
  • ✅ Time ও Place-এর শব্দ পরিবর্তন হতে পারে।

📖 Types of Narration

Narration প্রধানত দুই (2) প্রকার।

💬

Direct Speech

বক্তার কথা হুবহু Quotation (" ") এর ভিতরে লেখা হয়।

📝

Indirect Speech

বক্তার কথা নিজের ভাষায় অর্থ ঠিক রেখে প্রকাশ করা হয়।

💬 Direct Speech

যখন বক্তার কথাকে ঠিক যেমন বলা হয়েছে তেমনভাবে লেখা হয়, তখন তাকে Direct Speech বলে।

গঠন (Structure)

📝 Indirect Speech

যখন বক্তার কথাকে Quotation ছাড়া নিজের ভাষায় প্রকাশ করা হয়, তখন তাকে Indirect Speech বলে।

গঠন (Structure)

📘 Chapter 4 : Reporting Verb Rules

Narration Change করার সময় প্রথমে Reporting Verb লক্ষ্য করতে হবে। কারণ Reporting Verb অনুযায়ী Indirect Speech-এর Structure পরিবর্তিত হয়।

📖 What is Reporting Verb?

Quotation-এর আগে যে Verb থাকে তাকে Reporting Verb বলে।

Example

Rahim said, "I am happy."

এখানে said হলো Reporting Verb।

📝 Rule 1

Reporting Verb যদি Present Tense হয়

তাহলে সাধারণত Reported Speech-এর Tense পরিবর্তন হয় না।

Examples

  1. He says, "I am happy." → He says that he is happy.
  2. She says, "I know him." → She says that she knows him.
  3. Rahim says, "I play football." → Rahim says that he plays football.
  4. Mother says, "Drink milk." → Mother advises me to drink milk.
  5. Teacher says, "Read carefully." → Teacher advises us to read carefully.
  6. He says, "I have a pen." → He says that he has a pen.
  7. She says, "I like music." → She says that she likes music.
  8. They say, "We are ready." → They say that they are ready.
  9. Karim says, "I can swim." → Karim says that he can swim.
  10. Father says, "Work hard." → Father advises me to work hard.

📝 Rule 2

Reporting Verb যদি Past Tense হয়

তাহলে সাধারণত Reported Speech-এর Tense Back Shift হয়।

Examples

  1. He said, "I am happy." → He said that he was happy.
  2. She said, "I know him." → She said that she knew him.
  3. Rahim said, "I play football." → Rahim said that he played football.
  4. He said, "I have finished." → He said that he had finished.
  5. She said, "I can swim." → She said that she could swim.
  6. They said, "We shall win." → They said that they would win.
  7. Karim said, "I wrote a letter." → Karim said that he had written a letter.
  8. Father said, "Work hard." → Father advised me to work hard.
  9. Teacher said, "Be honest." → Teacher advised us to be honest.
  10. Mother said, "Don't waste time." → Mother advised me not to waste time.

💡 Shortcut Tips

  • ✅ says → Tense সাধারণত পরিবর্তন হয় না।
  • ✅ said → Tense সাধারণত পরিবর্তন হয়।
  • ✅ told এর পরে Object থাকে।
  • ✅ say এর পরে সাধারণত Object থাকে না।
  • ✅ Imperative Sentence-এ said → advised/ordered/requested হতে পারে।

📘 Rule 7

👤 Pronoun Change Rules

Narration Change-এর সবচেয়ে গুরুত্বপূর্ণ নিয়মগুলোর একটি হলো Pronoun Change। Pronoun পরিবর্তনের জন্য একটি সহজ সূত্র মনে রাখুন—

S.O.N Rule

  • S = Speaker (First Person)
  • O = Object (Second Person)
  • N = No Change (Third Person)

📖 Rule 1 : First Person

First Person (I, We, Me, Us, My, Our, Mine, Ours) সবসময় Subject (Speaker) অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তিত হয়।

Examples

  1. He said, "I am happy." → He said that he was happy.
  2. She said, "I know him." → She said that she knew him.
  3. Rahim said, "I play cricket." → Rahim said that he played cricket.
  4. Rina said, "I like tea." → Rina said that she liked tea.
  5. They said, "We are ready." → They said that they were ready.
  6. Father said, "I shall return." → Father said that he would return.
  7. Mother said, "I cook every day." → Mother said that she cooked every day.
  8. Karim said, "My brother is a doctor." → Karim said that his brother was a doctor.
  9. Rina said, "My mother is kind." → Rina said that her mother was kind.
  10. The boys said, "We won the match." → The boys said that they had won the match.

📖 Rule 2 : Second Person

Second Person (You, Your, Yours) সবসময় Object অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তিত হয়।

Examples

  1. He said to me, "You are honest." → He told me that I was honest.
  2. Father said to me, "You should study." → Father advised me that I should study.
  3. Teacher said to us, "You must work hard." → Teacher advised us that we had to work hard.
  4. Mother said to Rina, "You are late." → Mother told Rina that she was late.
  5. Karim said to Rahim, "You are my friend." → Karim told Rahim that he was his friend.
  6. She said to me, "You helped me." → She told me that I had helped her.
  7. He said to them, "You are lucky." → He told them that they were lucky.
  8. Father said to us, "You are my children." → Father told us that we were his children.
  9. The teacher said to me, "You can do it." → The teacher told me that I could do it.
  10. Mother said to the boys, "You should sleep early." → Mother advised the boys that they should sleep early.

📖 Rule 3 : Third Person

Third Person (He, She, They, It) সাধারণত কোনো পরিবর্তন হয় না।

Examples

  1. He said, "She is my sister." → He said that she was his sister.
  2. Rahim said, "They are honest." → Rahim said that they were honest.
  3. She said, "He is my uncle." → She said that he was her uncle.
  4. They said, "It is raining." → They said that it was raining.
  5. Father said, "They are waiting." → Father said that they were waiting.
  6. Mother said, "She is reading." → Mother said that she was reading.
  7. Karim said, "He knows me." → Karim said that he knew him.
  8. Rina said, "They have arrived." → Rina said that they had arrived.
  9. The boy said, "She sings well." → The boy said that she sang well.
  10. The teacher said, "They are absent." → The teacher said that they were absent.

Person Rule

💡 Shortcut Tricks

  • ✅ First Person → Subject অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তন।
  • ✅ Second Person → Object অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তন।
  • ✅ Third Person → সাধারণত পরিবর্তন হয় না।
  • ✅ S.O.N. Rule মনে রাখলে Pronoun Change সহজ হয়ে যায়।

🕒 Time & Place Change Rules

Narration Change-এ Reporting Verb যদি Past Tense হয়, তাহলে শুধু Tense নয়, অনেক সময় Time এবং Place-এর শব্দও পরিবর্তন করতে হয়।

⚠️ Important Notes

  • ✅ সব বাক্যে Time Change হবে না।
