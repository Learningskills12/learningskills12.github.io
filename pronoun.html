📖 Pronoun কী?

যে Word, Noun-এর পরিবর্তে ব্যবহৃত হয় তাকে Pronoun বলে।

অর্থাৎ, একই Noun বারবার ব্যবহার না করে তার পরিবর্তে Pronoun ব্যবহার করা হয়।

📚 উদাহরণ

Sentence Pronoun
Rahim is a good boy. He reads every day. He
Sima is a student. She goes to school. She
The book is new. It is interesting. It
Rina and Mina are friends. They play together. They

📚 Types of Pronoun (Pronoun-এর প্রকারভেদ)

Type Definition (সংজ্ঞা) Example
Personal Pronoun ব্যক্তির পরিবর্তে ব্যবহৃত Pronoun। I, We, You, He, She, It, They
Possessive Pronoun মালিকানা বা অধিকার বোঝায়। Mine, Yours, His, Hers, Ours, Theirs
Reflexive Pronoun কর্তা নিজের কাজ নিজের ওপর করলে ব্যবহৃত হয়। Myself, Yourself, Himself, Herself
Demonstrative Pronoun কোনো ব্যক্তি বা বস্তুকে নির্দেশ করে। This, That, These, Those
Interrogative Pronoun প্রশ্ন করার জন্য ব্যবহৃত হয়। Who, Whom, Whose, Which, What
Relative Pronoun দুটি বাক্যকে সংযুক্ত করে এবং পূর্ববর্তী Noun-কে নির্দেশ করে। Who, Whom, Which, That, Whose
Indefinite Pronoun অনির্দিষ্ট ব্যক্তি বা বস্তুকে বোঝায়। Someone, Anybody, Everyone, Nobody
Distributive Pronoun একটি একটি করে ব্যক্তি বা বস্তুকে বোঝায়। Each, Either, Neither

💡 সহজে মনে রাখার কৌশল

  • 👤 Personal = ব্যক্তি বোঝায়
  • 👑 Possessive = মালিকানা বোঝায়
  • 🪞 Reflexive = নিজের কাজ নিজের ওপর
  • 👉 Demonstrative = নির্দেশ করে
  • ❓ Interrogative = প্রশ্ন করে
  • 🔗 Relative = দুটি বাক্য যুক্ত করে
  • 🌍 Indefinite = অনির্দিষ্ট ব্যক্তি/বস্তু
  • 1️⃣ Distributive = একটি একটি করে বোঝায়

📝 আরও কিছু উদাহরণ

Sentence Pronoun Type
He is my friend. Personal Pronoun
This is my book. Demonstrative Pronoun
Who is calling? Interrogative Pronoun
The boy who won the prize is my brother. Relative Pronoun
Everyone enjoyed the program. Indefinite Pronoun
Each of the students received a certificate. Distributive Pronoun

📖 Important Rules of Pronoun

✅ Rule 1: Pronoun অবশ্যই তার Noun-এর Number ও Gender অনুযায়ী হবে।

Example:

  • ✔ Rahim is a student. He studies hard.
  • ✔ Rina is a doctor. She works in a hospital.
  • ✔ The boys are playing. They are happy.

✅ Rule 2: Pronoun-এর আগে Noun স্পষ্টভাবে উল্লেখ থাকতে হবে।

Example:

  • ✔ Karim bought a bicycle. He likes it.
  • ✔ Mina has a cat. It is very cute.

✅ Rule 3: বস্তু বা প্রাণীর জন্য সাধারণত It ব্যবহৃত হয়।

Example:

  • ✔ The book is interesting. It is very useful.
  • ✔ The cat is sleeping. It is under the table.

✅ Rule 4: একাধিক ব্যক্তি বোঝালে They ব্যবহার করা হয়।

Example:

  • ✔ Rahim and Karim are friends. They play football.
  • ✔ The students are in the classroom. They are studying.

✅ Rule 5: Reflexive Pronoun কর্তার নিজের কাজ নিজের উপর বোঝায়।

Example:

  • ✔ I did the work myself.
  • ✔ She completed the project herself.
  • ✔ They enjoyed themselves.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ ভুল ✅ সঠিক
Rahim said that she is ill. Rahim said that he is ill.
The girls lost his books. The girls lost their books.
The dog wagged his tail. The dog wagged its tail.
Me went to school. I went to school.

💡 Easy Tips

  • 📌 He = ছেলে বা পুরুষ
  • 📌 She = মেয়ে বা নারী
  • 📌 It = বস্তু বা প্রাণী
  • 📌 They = একাধিক ব্যক্তি বা বস্তু
  • 📌 We = আমরা
  • 📌 I = আমি
  • 📌 You = তুমি / আপনারা
  • 📌 Pronoun ব্যবহার করলে একই Noun বারবার লিখতে হয় না।

❓ Pronoun MCQ (১–২০)

  1. A Pronoun is used instead of a ________.
    A) Verb
    B) Adjective
    C) Noun
    D) Adverb
    উত্তর: C) Noun

  2. Which one is a Personal Pronoun?
    A) He
    B) This
    C) Which
    D) Each
    উত্তর: A) He

  3. Which is a Possessive Pronoun?
    A) Mine
    B) Me
    C) Him
    D) Them
    উত্তর: A) Mine

  4. Which is a Reflexive Pronoun?
    A) Himself
    B) His
    C) Him
    D) He
    উত্তর: A) Himself

  5. Which is a Demonstrative Pronoun?
    A) This
    B) Who
    C) Mine
    D) Myself
    উত্তর: A) This

  6. Which is an Interrogative Pronoun?
    A) What
    B) Those
    C) Their
    D) Ours
    উত্তর: A) What

  7. Which is a Relative Pronoun?
    A) Who
    B) They
    C) It
    D) We
    উত্তর: A) Who

  8. Which is an Indefinite Pronoun?
    A) Someone
    B) This
    C) He
    D) Myself
    উত্তর: A) Someone

  9. Which is a Distributive Pronoun?
    A) Each
    B) We
    C) They
    D) She
    উত্তর: A) Each

  10. Choose the correct pronoun:
    Rahim is a student. ____ reads every day.
    A) She
    B) It
    C) He
    D) They
    উত্তর: C) He

  11. The girls are playing. ____ are happy.
    A) He
    B) They
    C) It
    D) She
    উত্তর: B) They

  12. The cat is sleeping. ____ is under the chair.
    A) He
    B) They
    C) It
    D) We
    উত্তর: C) It

  13. I completed the work ______.
    A) Himself
    B) Myself
    C) Yourself
    D) Herself
    উত্তর: B) Myself

  14. Which sentence is correct?
    A) Me am a student.
    B) I am a student.
    C) Myself am a student.
    D) Mine am a student.
    উত্তর: B) I am a student.

  15. Which Pronoun shows ownership?
    A) Mine
    B) He
    C) This
    D) Who
    উত্তর: A) Mine

  16. Which Pronoun is used for things?
    A) She
    B) They
    C) It
    D) He
    উত্তর: C) It

  17. Which Pronoun is used to ask questions?
    A) Which
    B) They
    C) We
    D) Him
    উত্তর: A) Which

  18. Which sentence has a Relative Pronoun?
    A) This is my book.
    B) The boy who won the race is my friend.
    C) They are students.
    D) She is a doctor.
    উত্তর: B) The boy who won the race is my friend.

  19. Pronouns help to avoid ________.
    A) Reading
    B) Repetition of Nouns
    C) Writing
    D) Listening
    উত্তর: B) Repetition of Nouns

  20. How many main types of Pronoun are commonly taught?
    A) 5
    B) 6
    C) 8
    D) 10
    উত্তর: C) 8
