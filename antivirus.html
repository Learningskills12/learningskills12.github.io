🛡️ Types of Antivirus

🦠

Signature Based

Known viruses are detected using virus signatures stored in the antivirus database.

⚡

Heuristic Based

Detects new or unknown malware by analyzing suspicious behavior.

☁️

Cloud Antivirus

Uses cloud servers to scan files quickly and reduce system load.

🤖

AI Antivirus

Uses Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to identify modern cyber threats.

⭐ Features of Antivirus

✔️

Virus Detection

Scans files and removes viruses automatically.

🔥

Real-Time Protection

Monitors your computer continuously and blocks threats instantly.

🌐

Web Protection

Blocks dangerous websites and phishing attacks.

📧

Email Protection

Scans email attachments before opening them.

✅ Advantages of Antivirus

🛡️

Protects Against Malware

Detects and removes viruses, worms, trojans, spyware and ransomware.

⚡

Real-Time Protection

Monitors files continuously and blocks threats before they can infect your device.

🌍

Safe Internet Browsing

Warns users about phishing websites and unsafe downloads.

📧

Email Security

Scans email attachments and prevents malicious files from opening.

❌ Disadvantages of Antivirus

🐢

Can Slow Down the System

Background scanning may reduce performance on low-end computers.

💰

Premium Features Cost Money

Advanced security features are often available only in paid versions.

🔄

Needs Regular Updates

Without updates, new malware may not be detected.
