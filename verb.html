📖 Verb কী?

যে Word দ্বারা কোনো কাজ (Action), অবস্থা (State) অথবা ঘটনা (Occurrence) বোঝায়, তাকে Verb বলে।

সহজভাবে বললে, যে শব্দ দ্বারা কোনো কাজ করা বা হওয়া বোঝায়, সেটিই Verb।

📚 উদাহরণ

Sentence Verb
Rahim plays football. plays
She reads every day. reads
They are happy. are
The baby is sleeping. is sleeping

📚 Types of Verb (Verb-এর প্রকারভেদ)

Type Definition (সংজ্ঞা) Example
Main Verb যে Verb বাক্যের প্রধান কাজ বোঝায়। play, read, write, eat
Helping Verb (Auxiliary Verb) যে Verb প্রধান Verb-কে সাহায্য করে। is, am, are, was, were, have, has, had
Transitive Verb যে Verb-এর অর্থ সম্পূর্ণ করতে Object লাগে। He reads a book.
Intransitive Verb যে Verb-এর পরে Object লাগে না। The baby sleeps.
Finite Verb যে Verb Subject ও Tense অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তিত হয়। He plays football.
Non-finite Verb যে Verb Subject বা Tense অনুযায়ী পরিবর্তিত হয় না। To play, Playing, Played

💡 সহজে মনে রাখার কৌশল

  • 🏃 Main Verb = মূল কাজ
  • 🤝 Helping Verb = মূল Verb-কে সাহায্য করে
  • 📖 Transitive Verb = Object লাগে
  • 🚶 Intransitive Verb = Object লাগে না
  • ⏰ Finite Verb = Tense অনুযায়ী বদলায়
  • ♾️ Non-finite Verb = Tense অনুযায়ী বদলায় না

📝 আরও কিছু উদাহরণ

Sentence Verb Type
She writes a letter. Transitive Verb
The birds fly. Intransitive Verb
He is reading. Helping Verb + Main Verb
They play football. Finite Verb
To learn English is important. Non-finite Verb (Infinitive)

📖 Important Rules of Verb

✅ Rule 1: প্রতিটি সম্পূর্ণ বাক্যে একটি Finite Verb থাকে।

Example:

  • ✔ He plays football.
  • ✔ She reads a book.
  • ✔ They are happy.

✅ Rule 2: Subject একবচন হলে Singular Verb এবং বহুবচন হলে Plural Verb ব্যবহার করতে হয়।

Example:

  • ✔ He plays cricket.
  • ✔ She writes a letter.
  • ✔ They play cricket.
  • ✔ We write letters.

✅ Rule 3: Helping Verb সবসময় Main Verb-কে সাহায্য করে।

Example:

  • ✔ He is reading.
  • ✔ They have finished the work.
  • ✔ She was sleeping.

✅ Rule 4: Transitive Verb-এর পরে Object থাকে।

Example:

  • ✔ I eat rice.
  • ✔ She wrote a letter.
  • ✔ They watched a movie.

✅ Rule 5: Intransitive Verb-এর পরে Object থাকে না।

Example:

  • ✔ The baby cried.
  • ✔ Birds fly.
  • ✔ He slept well.

✅ Rule 6: Infinitive সাধারণত to + Verb (Base Form) হয়।

Example:

  • ✔ To read is a good habit.
  • ✔ I want to learn English.
  • ✔ She likes to sing.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ ভুল ✅ সঠিক
He go to school. He goes to school.
She don't like tea. She doesn't like tea.
They is playing. They are playing.
I has a pen. I have a pen.
He have finished. He has finished.

💡 Easy Tips

  • 📌 প্রতিটি বাক্যে Verb খুঁজে বের করার অভ্যাস করুন।
  • 📌 He, She, It-এর সাথে সাধারণ বর্তমান কালে Verb-এর শেষে s/es যোগ হয়।
  • 📌 I, We, You, They-এর সাথে Verb-এর Base Form ব্যবহৃত হয়।
  • 📌 Helping Verb এবং Main Verb-এর পার্থক্য ভালোভাবে শিখুন।
  • 📌 Verb ভালোভাবে শিখলে Tense, Voice Change ও Narration অনেক সহজ হয়ে যাবে।

❓ Verb MCQ (১–২০)

  1. What is a Verb?
    A) A naming word
    B) An action or state word
    C) A describing word
    D) A joining word
    উত্তর: B) An action or state word

  2. Which one is a Verb?
    A) Book
    B) Beautiful
    C) Run
    D) Honesty
    উত্তর: C) Run

  3. Choose the Verb:
    She sings beautifully.
    A) She
    B) Sings
    C) Beautifully
    D) None
    উত্তর: B) Sings

  4. Which is a Helping Verb?
    A) Read
    B) Play
    C) Is
    D) Write
    উত্তর: C) Is

  5. Which sentence has a Transitive Verb?
    A) Birds fly.
    B) He sleeps.
    C) She wrote a letter.
    D) They laughed.
    উত্তর: C) She wrote a letter.

  6. Which sentence has an Intransitive Verb?
    A) He kicked the ball.
    B) She reads a book.
    C) The baby cried.
    D) They bought a car.
    উত্তর: C) The baby cried.

  7. Which is a Finite Verb?
    A) Playing
    B) To read
    C) Reads
    D) Written
    উত্তর: C) Reads

  8. Which is a Non-finite Verb?
    A) Reads
    B) Played
    C) Playing
    D) Goes
    উত্তর: C) Playing

  9. Choose the correct sentence.
    A) He go to school.
    B) He goes to school.
    C) He going to school.
    D) He gone to school.
    উত্তর: B) He goes to school.

  10. Which Helping Verb is used with "I"?
    A) Is
    B) Are
    C) Am
    D) Were
    উত্তর: C) Am

  11. They ____ football every day.
    A) Plays
    B) Play
    C) Playing
    D) Played
    উত্তর: B) Play

  12. She ____ a letter now.
    A) Write
    B) Writes
    C) Is writing
    D) Written
    উত্তর: C) Is writing

  13. Which sentence is correct?
    A) They is happy.
    B) They are happy.
    C) They am happy.
    D) They has happy.
    উত্তর: B) They are happy.

  14. The infinitive form is:
    A) Reading
    B) Reads
    C) To read
    D) Read
    উত্তর: C) To read

  15. Which sentence contains a Main Verb only?
    A) He runs fast.
    B) He is running.
    C) She has gone.
    D) They were playing.
    উত্তর: A) He runs fast.

  16. Which verb agrees with "She"?
    A) Go
    B) Goes
    C) Going
    D) Gone
    উত্তর: B) Goes

  17. Which sentence is correct?
    A) I has a pen.
    B) I have a pen.
    C) I having a pen.
    D) I hads a pen.
    উত্তর: B) I have a pen.

  18. Which one is NOT a Verb?
    A) Eat
    B) Sleep
    C) Honest
    D) Write
    উত্তর: C) Honest

  19. A Verb expresses ________.
    A) Colour only
    B) Name only
    C) Action or State
    D) Number only
    উত্তর: C) Action or State

  20. Every complete sentence must have at least one ________.
    A) Adjective
    B) Noun
    C) Verb
    D) Adverb
    উত্তর: C) Verb
